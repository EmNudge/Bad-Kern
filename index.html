This is a joke bookmarklet

After being directed to this site over here, I was inspired to whip up a quick bookmarklet to do this horrible atrocity to any paragraph of your choosing.

This bookmarklet works by targeting all <p> tags on a page and, if granted permission, randomly changes the margin on each character in a word, excluding the first and last letter.

Bad Kern A Paragraph

You can drag that button/link to the navbar in order to activate it later, or click it now to see its effects on this page.
